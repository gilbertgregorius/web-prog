About Us

We serves a pivotal role, acting as the heart and hands of Christ’s mission within the academic community. It is dedicated to fostering a spirit of service and compassion, echoing the teachings of Christ through various community-oriented activities and services.

This division not only seeks to nurture the spiritual growth of its members but also strives to impact the wider community positively, embodying the love and values taught by Christ.

By organizing events, outreach programs, and service projects, the ministry division provides a platform for students to engage in meaningful work that promotes social justice, peace, and understanding, thereby translating faith into action.

Blood Donation

Redeeming Love

A seminar focused on the exploration of finding a life partner, guided by principles of love, respect, and mutual understanding, often drawing from religious or spiritual teachings.

Easter Care

A charitable outreach that takes place around Easter, providing aid and support to those in need, often including activities like distributing food, clothing, and offering companionship.

Internal Spiritual Revival Service

An event aimed at deepening the spiritual life of the members within the organization, through worship, prayer, and reflection, fostering a stronger internal community bond.

External Spiritual Revival Service

Similar to the internal service, this event reaches out to the external community, inviting non-members to participate in spiritual activities, promoting inclusivity and spiritual growth.

Redeeming Minds

A seminar that encourages attendees to renew their mindset according to Biblical teachings, focusing on personal growth, positive thinking, and aligning one’s life with spiritual values.
